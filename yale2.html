Yale Fight Songs

While we are the band and not the Glee Club, most of us do know the lyrics to Yale’s various fight songs and will sing them at the top of our collective lungs when they’re not being used to play our instruments. Sometimes we like to sing and play at the same time for an added challenge. Some sections are better than others at this.

“Yale” is considered to be the Yale Fight Song, the one we play after touchdowns during a football game. “Yale” is played at the end of every Yale athletic event, win or lose. “Yale” is our tear-jerking alma mater.
